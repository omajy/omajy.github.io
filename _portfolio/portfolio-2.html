I sourced a student dataset from Kaggle and used Pandas to pre-process and understand the data fields including name, nationalility, city, age, and grades in English, maths, science, and language.

I analysed the top-performing demographics in each subject and explored the correlations between these factors and academic performance, visualising the findings using Matplotlib and Seaborn.

This was my first python data analsyis and machine learning project. I chose to use the scikit-learn library, training a Random Forest Regressor to predict certain grades based on an input of demographic data, and tthen assessing its performance and the limitations of the dataset and challenges I faced.
